\section{Overview}

\begin{frame}{The Roadmap: From Algebra to Geometry}
    \textbf{Main Goal:} To demonstrate how the classification of semi-simple Lie algebras reduces to the geometric tessellation of a Euclidean space.

    \vspace{0.3cm}
    \textbf{Logical Path (30 Minutes):}
    \begin{enumerate}
        \item \textbf{The Foundation:} Assume the Cartan-Weyl decomposition and the Euclidean nature of the root space.
        \item \textbf{The Geometric Constraint:} Derive the integer quantization of angles (Crystallographic condition).
        \item \textbf{Rank-2 Classification:} Visually construct the only 4 allowed 2D root systems ($A_1 \oplus A_1, A_2, B_2, G_2$).
        \item \textbf{Dynkin Diagrams \& The End Result:} Define simple roots, extract Dynkin diagrams, and state the final classification (ADE and Exceptionals), including accidental isomorphisms.
    \end{enumerate}
\end{frame}
